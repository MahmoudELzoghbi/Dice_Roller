How to play the gameüòç

  1. The first player rolls the dice to get a random result between 1 and 6.
  2. If the number 1 appears, the player loses all the points they have accumulated so far and the turn moves to the next player.
  3. If a number other than 1 appears, the points obtained from rolling the dice are added to the player's total score.
  4. The dice is then passed to the next player, who repeats the process.
  5. Players continue rolling the dice and accumulating points until one of them reaches 100 points.
  6. You can repeat the game to determine consecutive winners.
